# bing2fa
### Autofill Binghamton University Two-Factor Authentication

Tired of checking your authenticator app every 30 minutes to login to your BU account? Fret no more. With bing2fa, you will never need to input your OTP again. The extension will automatically enter the code as soon as the page comes up, no interaction required.

## Setup
1. Download the extension from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/bggmcalehpfhejdoleaeijedpchhlcdj) or [Mozilla Addons](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/bing2fa/)
2. Go to https://password.binghamton.edu, click *"I know my username / password / one-time-password (OTP) and I want to configure my second factor authentication (2FA)"*, and login
3. Click *"Create New Token"*, make sure you choose Authenticator App, and name it whatever you want
4. Click "OK" on the bing2fa prompt
5. You're done!

*Note: This does not remove the protection of 2FA from your account, as it simply locally stores your 2FA key within your browser storage. This will not sync between different devices or browser profiles.*
